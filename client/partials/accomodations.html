A few more details

  • Cutoff date: You have until 02/05/2017 to make the room reservations. After that, the rooms and rates are subject to availability.
  • The first night’s room and tax is required at the time of booking as a deposit
  • Guest must present the card used for the deposit at check-in or we will need to refund the deposit at check-in.
  • Cancellation Policy: Guests may cancel up to 72 hours prior to their arrival, without penalty. Inside 72 hours, the deposit will be kept
  • One (1) rollaway per room is available in select rooms at an additional per day cost.
  • A rollaway bed is not permitted in a room that already has two queen beds.
  • Outside food and beverage is prohibited within all areas of the hotel.
  • The main guest must be 21 years old to check in.
  • Our check in time starts at 3pm, check out time is 11am.
  • Upon check in, each room is required to put down a credit card for incidental charges – its $150 authorization per day, plus any outstanding room and tax.
  • Resort fee of $30 per day, per room, plus tax is charged upon check in.
